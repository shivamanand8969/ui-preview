    • About CORE CREDIT SERVICES

    CORE CREDIT SERVICES is a leading collection partner for top companies in the industry, offering reliable and efficient services across various sectors.

    Company Overview

    CORE CREDIT SERVICES is registered under the Registrar of Firms, Service Tax, and Professional Tax. We started our operations in August 2002 with just 5 field executives and 2 telecalling executives, primarily with Vodafone as the principal company. Since then, we have expanded our business to include major corporations like Bharti Airtel Ltd, Bajaj Finserve, Fullerton India, Aditya Birla Finance Ltd, and RBL Credit Cards.

    Today, we have a dedicated team of 40 field executives, 45 tele-callers, 4 back-office support staff, and 10 team leaders handling various divisions. Our commitment to performance and meeting targets is what drives us to continually excel in our field. Our vision is to meet and exceed the standards set by our principals and achieve exceptional results.

    We pride ourselves on having a team of well-experienced and educated field executives who are a valuable asset to our company, contributing with their hard work and dedication to the success of the organization.

    Our Workplace

    We are well-equipped with a fully furnished and automated office spanning 1500 sqft, providing a great working atmosphere. Our office is centrally located, with all important locations around the city falling within a radius of 5 kilometers.
