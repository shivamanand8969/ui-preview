cB Labs

“Quality comes from Experience, not from a Degree”

At the Codebucket Lab our focus is to train college students in learning various industry important skills and mentoring them for their better future. The rigorous training lasts for 2 months where the student are taught applications of Data Analytics, different ML and AI algorithms and DevOps techniques by our core team members absolutely free of cost. At the end of the training a hackathon is conducted and the top performers are awarded with opportunity to work with Codebucket.
